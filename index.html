Curious Questions of Poor Smeagol

Sméagol lived in his cosy cave within Misty Mountains.
He had been often visited by various strangers from all over Middle-Earth.
As he was rather chatty Stoor representative, Sméagol was head over heels to be inquisitive towards every guest,
who happened to visit his cave.
However due to his quite lonely existence,
Sméagol had tendency to inquire odd questions on occasion.




As Smeagol's guests, wanderers were wont to be polite to an extent...
Each of them politeness treated vastly differently.
Some were rude, some inadequate in their answers, others were silly in response,
so searching for any meaning frequently was in vain.
Neverthless, all as one men were willing to give an answer to Sméagol's questions.
